The problem

Small shops do not need a monster ERP.

Running a small shop means dealing with stock every day. Products arrive. Invoices pile up. Some items have expiry dates, some do not. Stock levels change. Something gets moved, corrected, counted again, or found on a shelf where it apparently decided to start a new life.

Spreadsheets can help for a while. Then they become another thing to manage. All-G StockMate is being built for the space between messy spreadsheets and oversized enterprise systems.

What it helps with

Keep your shop stock clear, practical, and usable.

Product database

Manage product names, categories, supplier data, EAN/GTIN, producer codes, and shop-specific details.

Stock levels

Track what is in stock, what is running low, and what needs attention before it becomes retail theatre.

Deliveries and batches

Add deliveries, connect them with products, and keep received stock under better control.

Expiry dates

Handle known dates, missing dates, no date information, or products where expiry dates do not apply.

Stocktaking

Run basic stocktaking, correct stock levels, and make daily stock work more transparent.

CSV import/export

Import product data from CSV files. Export your product base when you need it elsewhere.

Transfers

Standard will support stock transfers between small shops or storage points.

Daily filters

Find low stock, missing stock, short expiry dates, expired products, and items that need review.

FAQ

Questions normal people might ask before trusting another piece of software.

What is All-G StockMate?

All-G StockMate is a practical inventory tool for small retail shops. It helps manage products, stock levels, deliveries, batches, expiry dates, stocktaking, imports, exports, and transfers.

Is All-G StockMate only for pet shops?

No. The first real-world preset comes from a pet shop, but the product is designed for small retail shops in general.

What is included in EA Free?

EA Free includes one location, product database, basic stock control, stocktaking, corrections, CSV import/export, basic filters, and a product limit.

What is included in EA Standard?

EA Standard is planned for 2+ locations, stock transfers, deliveries and batches, expiry dates and statuses, extended stocktaking, company settings, and fuller stock management.

Can I import products from CSV?

CSV import/export is part of the planned core workflow, especially for shops moving from spreadsheets.

Will All-G StockMate include AI features?

AI-assisted features are later-stage ideas, especially invoice reading, product matching, and workflow automation. First priority: a reliable stock system that does not need magic to be useful.

Does the Early Access form send my data anywhere?

No. This static MVP only shows the intended form experience. Real sending is not connected yet.
